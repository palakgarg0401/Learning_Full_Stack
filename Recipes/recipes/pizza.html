Veggie Pizza

Home

Veggie pizza is a delicious and colorful dish that combines a crispy crust with a variety of fresh, flavorful vegetables. Unlike traditional meat-heavy pizzas, veggie pizza focuses on wholesome ingredients like bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, tomatoes, and spinach, creating a vibrant and nutritious meal. The base is typically spread with tangy tomato sauce or creamy white sauce, followed by a generous layer of melted cheese, such as mozzarella or cheddar. Some versions also include unique toppings like artichokes, zucchini, or roasted garlic, adding even more depth to the flavors. The balance between the crunchy vegetables, gooey cheese, and savory sauce makes veggie pizza a favorite among vegetarians and pizza lovers alike.


One of the best things about veggie pizza is its versatility. It can be customized to suit different tastes and dietary preferences, whether it's made on a classic hand-tossed crust, a crispy thin crust, or even a gluten-free or whole wheat base. Seasonings like oregano, basil, red chili flakes, and garlic powder enhance the overall taste, while a drizzle of olive oil or balsamic glaze can add a gourmet touch. Veggie pizza is not only a healthier alternative to traditional pizza but also a great way to enjoy a variety of vegetables in a delicious and satisfying way. Whether baked at home or ordered from a pizzeria, it remains a timeless and crowd-pleasing dish.

-> Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (or vegan cheese)
  • Bell peppers (red, green, yellow)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives (black or green, sliced)
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Spinach or basil leaves
  • Oregano and chili flakes (for seasoning)
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)

-> Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking tray or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Evenly distribute the sliced veggies (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and tomatoes) on top.
  7. Add spinach or basil leaves for extra flavor.
  8. Season with oregano, chili flakes, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, then slice and serve hot.
